About Us

In data we trust

Data Science has been a growing technology in the last decade, and its use spread to cover different fields including schooling. Traditionally, students of every field struggle the most in finding internships that go with their career of interest. They start by searching companies in their specialties, and that for itself can consume a lot of time and energy.

Nevertheless, students can’t always find good online courses and certificates that can boost their chances of getting suitable opportunities. In addition, ESPRIT’s pedagogical responsibles cannot coordinate between the market needs and the number of students in each field. All these fastidious and long processes can be very exhausting and wearing for the students and ESPRIT's pedagogical responsibles, and as a consequence, it becomes prone to human errors.

  • Increase matching specific internship opportunities for students by 20%
  • Better management of resources and finances on different specialities for ESPRIT by 15%
  • Decrease the number of unnecessary interviews.

Services

To reach the goals of our project, it is required to address the following problems

For students

We offer the possibility to students to have an idea about companies where they can have internships and first jobs matching opportunities with Alumni studetns via a smart recommandation system

For ESPRIT’s pedagogical responsibles

Professors can have from now on a precise intel on market needs and informations about Alumni students careers through interactive POWER BI Dashboarding

For companies

Providing accurate matchs for professional profiles to satisfy the companies needs
